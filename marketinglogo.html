Logo


Master Logo

The Sencha master logo should be used at all times and should be free from surrounding graphics. This is to ensure that the logo and brand are used in a consistent manner across all products.

Background Color

When in doubt, use a white background behind the logo. When using a dark background, use white for the Sencha text.


Logo Placement

The Sencha logo should be placed in the upper left corner (when used in header), lower left corner (footer), or centered.

Improper Use of Logo

The logo cannot be changed to another color



The Sencha leaf cannot be separated from the text


The logo must not be skewed or rotated



A tagline cannot be added to the logo
