Buttons


Purpose

Buttons are used as triggers for actions. Depending on the use case, buttons contain a label and/or an icon. There are the types of buttons that are used in our products: Basic buttons, toolbar buttons, segmented buttons, dropdown buttons, split buttons, and icon buttons.
