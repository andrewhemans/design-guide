Logo


Master Logo

The Sencha master logo should be used at all times. Where not possible, one of the logo variations should be used. This is to ensure that the logo and brand are used in a consistent manner across all products.

Background Color

When in doubt, use a white background behind the logo. When using a dark background, use white for the Sencha text.


Logo Variations

The Sencha leaf logo is available in different colors. One of these logos must be present in all Sencha products. It must be visible in the header of the application as well as the app icon.
